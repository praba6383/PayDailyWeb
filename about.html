About PayDaily

Empowering businesses with secure, reliable, and innovative payment solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PayDaily emerged from a simple vision: to make digital payments accessible, secure, and seamless for businesses of all sizes. We recognized the growing need for reliable payment infrastructure in India's rapidly digitizing economy.

Today, we serve thousands of merchants across various industries, processing millions of transactions monthly with industry-leading security and reliability standards.

How Payment Gateway Works

Customer Initiates Payment

Customer selects products/services and proceeds to checkout on merchant's website or app.

Secure Data Transmission

Payment details are encrypted and securely transmitted to PayDaily's payment gateway.

Bank Authorization

We forward the request to the customer's bank or payment provider for authorization.

Transaction Completion

Upon approval, funds are transferred and both merchant and customer receive confirmation.
