Frequently Asked Questions

How long does integration take?

Most integrations can be completed within 2-3 business days. Our technical team provides full support throughout the process.

What are the settlement timelines?

UPI payments are settled instantly (T+0), while card payments are typically settled within T+1 to T+3 business days.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees or monthly charges. You only pay for successful transactions based on our transparent pricing model.

What security measures do you have?

We are PCI DSS Level 1 compliant with 256-bit SSL encryption, fraud detection, and real-time monitoring for maximum security.
